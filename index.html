About Cave Castle

Cave Castle Homestay, set alongside the flowing waters of Haritheerthakkara, invites you into a world shaped by time, nature, and tradition. An ancient natural rock cave, stands as a silent witness to history, while a nearby natural waterfall fills the air with gentle rhythm. An organic vegetable farm adds a touch of earth and freshness, and a fun kids’ play area brings laughter into the landscape.

Just beyond, the sacred Theyyam temples bring alive the powerful stories, colours, and rituals of North Malabar’s rich cultural heritage. Surrounded by nature and village calm, Cave Castle is not just a place to stay—it is a journey into the soul of North Malabar
